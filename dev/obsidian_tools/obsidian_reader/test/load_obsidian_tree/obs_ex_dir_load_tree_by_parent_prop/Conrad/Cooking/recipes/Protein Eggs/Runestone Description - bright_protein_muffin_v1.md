recipe_id: bright_protein_muffin_v1
title: Bright Protein Muffins with Goat Cheese & Lemon
version: 1.0
date: 2025-06-23
goal: Practice folding whipped whites and layering bright flavors
tools:
  - muffin tin (metal)
  - zester
  - hand mixer
  - mixing bowls
  - spatula
ingredients:
  eggs:
    whole: 2
    extra_whites: 2
  cottage_cheese: "1/2 cup"
  goat_cheese: "2 tbsp"
  lemon_zest: "zest of 1 lemon"
  baking_powder: "1/4 tsp"
  cornstarch: "1 tsp"
  salt: "pinch"
steps:
  - Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Prepare muffin tin with spray or liners.
  - Separate 2 whole eggs; add the 2 extra whites to the whites bowl.
  - Whip all 4 egg whites to soft peaks with a pinch of salt.
  - In another bowl, blend yolks, cottage cheese, goat cheese, lemon zest, baking powder, and cornstarch.
  - Gently fold whipped whites into the cheese/yolk mixture until just combined.
  - Spoon mixture into prepared muffin tin.
  - Bake 15–18 minutes, until puffed and golden.
  - Let rest 1–2 minutes before serving.
skill_targets:
  - Folding whipped egg whites
  - Oven muffin baking
  - Flavor balancing: acid + dairy
tags: [michelin_protein_eggs, bright_variant, cooking_skill_growth]
