**Ingredients (for ~1 lb chicken thighs):**

- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
    
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
    
- 1 tsp ground cumin
    
- 1 tsp ground coriander
    
- 1 tsp paprika
    
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
    
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
    
- 1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
    
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
    
- 1-inch piece fresh ginger, grated
    
- Salt to taste
    

**Instructions:**  
Mix everything, coat the chicken, marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight = best). Sear or roast before combining with the sauce.
