# Proposed Versions
## 1. Ordered List of Recipes (Product Progression)

### **P1. Basic Pan-Fried Protein Eggs**

- **Ingredients**: Whole eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese
    
- **Skills**: Basic pan-frying
    
- **Method**: Blend all ingredients. Cook gently in a nonstick pan.
    
- **Notes**: High-protein, simple texture, baseline flavor.
    

---

### **P2. Pan-Fried + Miso (Umami Boost)**

- **Ingredients**: Eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, miso paste
    
- **Skills**: Blending with flavor additives
    
- **Method**: Blend all ingredients, pan-fry as in P1.
    
- **Flavor**: Subtle umami from miso paste
    

---

### **P3. Pan-Fried + Chive + Miso (Flavor Layering)**

- **Ingredients**: Eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, miso paste, chopped chives
    
- **Skills**: Herb integration, balanced seasoning
    
- **Method**: Blend all, pan-fry
    
- **Flavor**: Umami + fresh herb brightness
    

---

### **P4. Oven-Baked (Savory Base)**

- **Ingredients**: Eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese
    
- **Skills**: Muffin baking
    
- **Method**: Blend, pour into silicone muffin cups, bake at 350°F for ~15 mins
    
- **Flavor**: Plain muffin-style, no added flavor yet
    

---

### **P5. Baked + Baking Powder (Lift Introduction)**

- **Ingredients**: Eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, baking powder
    
- **Skills**: Leavening agents
    
- **Method**: Add powder to base and bake as above
    
- **Flavor**: Lightly aerated muffin structure
    

---

### **P6. Baked + Chive + Miso (Savory Combo)**

- **Ingredients**: Eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, chives, miso paste, baking powder
    
- **Skills**: Flavor layering with leavening
    
- **Method**: Blend all, bake in muffin cups
    
- **Flavor**: First full "savory" version
    

---

### **P7. Baked + Whipped Whites (Lift Upgrade)**

- **Ingredients**: As P6, but whip egg whites separately and fold into base
    
- **Skills**: Whipping and folding egg whites (NEW)
    
- **Method**: Whip, fold, bake
    
- **Flavor**: Lighter, souffle-like structure
    

---

### **P8. Baked + Goat Cheese + Lemon Zest (Bright Base)**

- **Ingredients**: Cottage cheese, goat cheese, egg yolks, whipped egg whites, lemon zest
    
- **Skills**: Zesting citrus, folding into aerated base
    
- **Method**: Fold zest and cheese into base, bake
    
- **Flavor**: Bright, creamy, high-toned
    

---

### **P9. Baked + Fresh Thyme + Cornstarch (Bright Complete)**

- **Ingredients**: All from P8 plus fresh thyme and cornstarch
    
- **Skills**: Balancing acid + herb + structure
    
- **Method**: Bake as above
    
- **Flavor**: Full "bright" muffin
    

---

### **P10. Baked + Smoked Paprika (Smoky Start)**

- **Ingredients**: Base + smoked paprika
    
- **Skills**: Balancing smoke notes
    
- **Method**: Bake as base
    
- **Flavor**: Warm, smoky profile
    

---

### **P11. Baked + Honey or Molasses (Sweet Layer)**

- **Ingredients**: P10 + molasses or honey
    
- **Skills**: Sweet/savory flavor balancing
    
- **Method**: Bake as before
    
- **Flavor**: Deeper smoky-sweet
    

---

### **P12. Baked + Corn (Smoky-Sweet Complete)**

- **Ingredients**: P11 + charred corn
    
- **Skills**: Textural layering
    
- **Method**: Bake as before
    
- **Flavor**: Full smoky-sweet profile
    

---

### **P13. Sous-Vide Preparation (Texture Experiment)**

- **Ingredients**: Choose any from P6–P9
    
- **Skills**: Sous-vide (NEW)
    
- **Method**: Sous-vide in jars or molds, finish under broiler
    
- **Flavor**: Creamy interior, crisp edge
    

---

### **P14. iSi Whip Prep Base Only**

- **Ingredients**: Fine-strained version of base from P6 or P9
    
- **Skills**: iSi siphon use (NEW)
    
- **Method**: Dispense into preheated molds, bake immediately
    
- **Flavor**: Dramatic lift and softness
    

---

### **P15. iSi Whip + Citrus Gel Topping (Final Form)**

- **Ingredients**: P14 + citrus gel (yuzu/lemon juice + agar or pectin)
    
- **Skills**: Topping prep and presentation
    
- **Method**: Gel applied post-bake
    
- **Flavor**: Haute-cuisine elegance
