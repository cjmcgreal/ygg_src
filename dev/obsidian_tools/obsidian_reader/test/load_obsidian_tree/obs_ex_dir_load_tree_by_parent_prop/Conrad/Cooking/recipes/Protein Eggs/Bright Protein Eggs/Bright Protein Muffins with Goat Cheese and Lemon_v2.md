**Bright Protein Muffins with Goat Cheese & Lemon**

**Ingredients (makes 6–8 muffins):**
- 2 whole eggs (separated)
- 2 additional egg whites
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese
- 1 inch length of a big log (~2 tbsp) goat cheese
- Zest of 2 lemon
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- Pinch of salt
- 1/4 tsp dried thyme
- 1/4 tsp fresh thyme
- 1 tsp fresh lemon juice

**Tools:**
- Mixing bowls (x2)
- Hand or stand mixer (for whites)
- Zester / microplane
- Muffin tin (non-silicone)
- Baking spray or paper liners

**Steps:**
1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C). Grease muffin tin or use liners.
2. **Separate the 4 whole eggs**, placing whites in one bowl, yolks in another.
3. **Whip all 4 egg whites** to soft peaks with a pinch of salt.
	1. Skill: [[Whipping Egg Whites]]
4. In a separate bowl, **blend together**:
    - 2 Egg yolks - discard the other two
    - Cottage cheese
    - Goat cheese
    - Lemon zest
    - Baking powder
    - Cornstarch
5. **Gently fold whipped whites** into the cheese/yolk mixture using a spatula. Do not overmix — keep it airy.
	1. Skill: [[Folding Whipped Egg Whites]]
6. **Spoon mixture** into prepared muffin tin.
7. **Bake for 15 minutes**, or until puffed and lightly golden.
8. Let rest 1–2 min before serving.
